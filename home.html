Why Most Outbound Teams Stay Fragile

Individual Performance Is Not a System.

Most early-stage B2B SaaS teams build their outbound motion around people — not process. The result is a motion that looks like it's working until a rep leaves, a manager changes, or a quarter goes sideways.

Your best rep carries 60% of the meetings. When they leave, pipeline collapses.

There's no consistent standard for who gets worked, how sequences run, or what qualifies as a real opportunity.

Your CRM is a graveyard of stale data and ad-hoc decisions made by whoever was in the seat last.

You're managing output — not diagnosing variance. You see the result but never the root cause.

The motion feels fragile because it is. The SDR Operating System was built to fix that.
